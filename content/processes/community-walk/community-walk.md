# Community Walk

A community walk is a participatory methodology where members of a community collectively explore their local environment to identify assets, challenges, and opportunities for change.

## Purpose

Community walks serve to:
- Build shared understanding of place and community context
- Engage diverse perspectives on local challenges and opportunities
- Create a foundation for collaborative problem-solving
- Document community knowledge and lived experiences

## Process

1. **Preparation**
   - Define the purpose and focus of the walk
   - Identify key areas to explore
   - Invite diverse community members
   - Brief participants on the methodology

2. **During the Walk**
   - Move through the community space together
   - Encourage observation and reflection
   - Document findings through notes, photos, or sketches
   - Listen to stories and perspectives from community members

3. **Reflection & Synthesis**
   - Gather the group to share observations
   - Identify patterns and themes
   - Create a collective map or representation
   - Extract insights for action planning

## Outcomes

- Shared understanding of community strengths and challenges
- Documentation of community assets and resources
- Collaborative insights for development initiatives
- Strengthened relationships within the community
