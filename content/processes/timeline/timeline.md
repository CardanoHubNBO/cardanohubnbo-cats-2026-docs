# Timeline

A timeline is a visual representation of events, activities, and milestones organized sequentially to show the evolution and progression of a project, initiative, or learning journey.

## Purpose

Timelines serve to:
- Create a shared understanding of project progression
- Document key milestones and decisions
- Track learning and emergence over time
- Enable reflection on past experiences
- Plan future activities based on patterns

## Components

1. **Key Events**
   - Major milestones or turning points
   - Decisions made
   - Significant learnings

2. **Activities & Outputs**
   - Workshops or gatherings
   - Deliverables
   - Community engagements

3. **Reflections & Insights**
   - Emergent themes
   - Adaptive changes
   - Evolving understanding

## Creating a Timeline

1. Gather information from participants about what happened and when
2. Sequence events chronologically or thematically
3. Add context and reflections on why events mattered
4. Visualize the timeline in a format accessible to the community
5. Share and iterate with stakeholders for accuracy and completeness

## Uses in Action-Learning

Timelines are particularly useful for:
- Documenting action-learning journeys
- Capturing emergent processes
- Creating institutional memory
- Supporting adaptive management
- Sharing progress with stakeholders
